JAYSON TATUM

From No.1 pic to ROTY canidate, July 11,2018

Jayson Tatum, at 6-foot-8 and 204 pounds, already has a great frame and it will only fill out with more muscle and added strength as time goes on. He will be a strong physical specimen in the NBA. However, he does not yet possess elite explosiveness or athleticism to go along with his impressive build. Tatum, for now, is best suited to operate offensively in isolation, be it in the post or on the perimeter. He is very comfortable in the post, where he excelled at Duke.

MARIO MCKINNEY

Underated Over-Achhiever, July 11,2018

6'1, Elite, Combo Guard Mario McKnney is one of the most efficient guards in the state. He was the underdog until he exploded his sophomore year at Vashon Highschool. With him being a shifty yet explosive guard, it makes him a reliable scout for college coaches. He has recieved more than 8 offer, Xavier being one of them.
